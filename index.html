Intro




My name is Parker Snyder. I studied ASL interpreting for four years at Goshen College and graduated in the year 2020. During this time, I experimented with a variety of settings to interpret in including classroom, platform, one-on-one, and performance interpreting. Near the end of my college career, I began to realize that my preferred field of work is information-based, small scale interpreting like in a college classroom. Sciences and maths are my specialty.
